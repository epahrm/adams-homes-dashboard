## 2. Your daily routine (top to bottom)

The dashboard is built so nothing rides on memory. Work it top-down each morning:

- **🔵 Follow-Ups Due** — your call list for today. Each seller lead rides a set sequence (call → call → text → 2nd mailer → final offer). It tells you *who* to contact, *how*, and *how overdue*.
  - Click **Log Touch ✓** after you reach out — it records the touch and advances the sequence.
  - **Snooze 3d** to push it, **Open** to jump into the deal.
- **🟠 Re-offer Due** — sellers who declined; the system re-surfaces them 30 days later so you can try again if the lot's still available.
- **🟢 New Opportunities** — every buy-box lot that's ready, with a green/yellow/red light and a suggested offer.
  - **Send Offer** opens the offer screen · **Hold** parks it · **Unsuitable** removes it for good.
- **New Leads** — anyone who scanned a postcard QR or submitted the seller page. These are hot — reach out fast.

---

## 3. Making an offer

1. Click **Send Offer** (or open any lead) → the **Offer Approval** screen.
2. It **auto-pulls the county record** (owner, mailing address, parcel #, acreage) and checks the lot is vacant.
3. Confirm the **utility type** (sets the stipend) and the **offer amount** (capped at your stipend).
4. If it's a **listed** deal, the **Listing Agent** card auto-fills and the **commission** defaults to 3% — confirm it.
5. Add any **lot premiums** (corner, oversized, etc.) and **notes to Elizabeth**.
6. Click **Preview Packet** to see the cover letter, then generate the offer.
7. **Order of signatures:** the **seller signs first**, then **Elizabeth counter-signs last**. Send via your **Dotloop** login for now.

---

## 4. Bringing in your own deals & contacts

Up in the pipeline toolbar:

- **+ Add Deal / Contract** — drop an existing or in-progress deal straight into the pipeline at the right stage.
- **Import Contacts (CSV)** — upload a mailer list or a spreadsheet of current deals. You'll get a **Review** screen showing exactly what will be added (and flagging any already in the system) — nothing is added until you click **Confirm & Add**.
  - Add a **`Stage`** column to your spreadsheet to land in-progress deals at their real pipeline stage.
- **Off-Market Sweep** — pulls vacant Palm Bay lots from county records for a blind-mailer campaign; preview the count, download the mailing list, or add them to your worklist.
- **Postcards / QR** — generate the postcard QR code and the mailing-list export for your print vendor.

## 5. Where to find your numbers

- **KPI tiles** up top: Pending EP Sig, IP, Manager Driven, Survey Rcv'd, CD Approved, CTC, Closed (this month).
- **Closing calendar** — this month + next, clickable.
- **Realtor CRM** — every listing agent you've worked with, ranked by volume.
- **Costs, ROI & Renewals** — mailing spend, cost per contract, commissions held, account renewals, and cancellations. (Mostly Elizabeth's view, but good to know it's there.)

## 6. If something looks off

- **Numbers not updating?** Refresh the page (top-right **Refresh Data**).
- **"Offline demo mode" banner?** That means it briefly couldn't reach the server — it'll reconnect on its own; refresh in a minute.
- **Anything else:** tell Elizabeth.
