## Getting around
Walk: yes — very walkable. Subway + walking was the winning combo;
rideshare only for airport runs. Bikes: Central Park rental was a
highlight. Safety: great everywhere EXCEPT Chinatown after dark (felt a
little sketchy — daylight only); twin nieces loved Times Square at night.

## Morning run (Elizabeth)
~4 miles every morning: the Two Bridges loop — Brooklyn Bridge +
Manhattan Bridge (verified: the classic loop is ~4 mi; Manhattan Bridge
confirmed as "the other bridge right there," landing at Chinatown) —
straight from the hotel door.

## Chill spot
Brookfield Place Winter Garden — the palm-filled glass atrium on the
Hudson by the Battery Park City waterfront — with the multi-story
Starbucks; went several times to relax. (Confirm exact Starbucks with
Elizabeth.)
