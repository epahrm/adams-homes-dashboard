## Customization Guide

**Before Sending, Replace:**
- `[EMPLOYEE_NAME]` → Employee's first name
- `[EMPLOYEE_EMAIL]` → Employee's email address
- `[TEMP_PASSWORD]` → Temporary password from system
- `[X]` → Your timeline (e.g., "30" for 30 days)
- `[ADMIN_NAME]` → Your name
- `[ADMIN_EMAIL]` → Your email
- `[ADMIN_PHONE]` → Your phone number

---

## Sending Tips

✅ **Best Practices:**
- Send on employee's first day
- Send from company email account (not Gmail personal)
- Use BCC if sending to multiple employees
- Follow up within 24 hours to confirm receipt
- Keep a copy for your records

❌ **Avoid:**
- Sending password in multiple emails (send in one secure message)
- Generic greeting (personalize with name)
- Making it feel like spam (keep it friendly and professional)
- Sending at odd hours (business hours preferred)
