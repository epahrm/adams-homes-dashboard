## PART 2: ADMIN DASHBOARD - STEP BY STEP INSTRUCTIONS

### How to Access the Admin Dashboard

1. **Go to the login page:** https://adams-homes-dashboard.vercel.app
2. **Click "Admin Login →"** (link at bottom of login page)
3. **Enter your admin credentials** (contact your administrator for access)
4. **Click "Login"** - You'll see the Admin Dashboard

### Admin Dashboard Overview

The Admin Dashboard has 4 main tabs:

#### TAB 1: 📊 DASHBOARD
**Purpose:** View company-wide onboarding metrics and progress

**Displays:**
- Total Sales Associates (number of team members)
- Completed Onboarding (how many finished all 8 milestones)
- In Progress (currently onboarding)
- Questions Pending (employee questions awaiting responses)

**What to do here:**
- Monitor overall progress at a glance
- Use this to identify which teams need support
- Track when team members are falling behind

---

#### TAB 2: 💬 QUESTIONS
**Purpose:** Review and respond to employee questions during onboarding

**How to use it:**
1. Click the "Questions" tab
2. You'll see a list of all questions from team members
3. Each question shows:
   - Employee name
   - Their division/location
   - The question they asked
   - Status: "Pending" (not answered) or "Answered" (already responded)
   - Date/time submitted

**What to do:**
- Review pending questions regularly (at least daily)
- Identify questions that need clarification
- **Note:** Response system coming soon - for now, respond to employees directly via email
- Share common questions with your team to reinforce learning

---

#### TAB 3: 👥 SALES ASSOCIATES

#### TAB 4: ⚙️ SETTINGS

## PART 3: HOW TO ADD A NEW SALES ASSOCIATE

### Method 1: Employee Self-Signup (Recommended)
This is the easiest way to add new team members.

**Steps:**
1. Provide employee with dashboard URL: https://adams-homes-dashboard.vercel.app
2. Tell them to click **"Sign Up"** on the login page
3. They'll enter:
   - Email address
   - Password (they choose)
   - Full name
   - Division/location
   - Hire date (optional)
4. Click "Sign Up" to create their account
5. They're automatically logged in and can start onboarding

**Advantages:**
- Employee sets their own password
- They can start immediately
- No passwords shared with admin

## PART 5: NEW TEAM MEMBER INSTRUCTIONS
### What Happens When You First Log In

### Step 1: Create Your Account
1. Go to: https://adams-homes-dashboard.vercel.app
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Fill in your information:
   - **Email:** Your work email
   - **Password:** Create a secure password (you'll need this to log in)
   - **Full Name:** Your name as you want it displayed
   - **Division:** Your office location
   - **Hire Date:** Your start date (optional)
4. Click **"Sign Up"**

### Step 2: Your Dashboard
Once logged in, you'll see:
- **Your Progress:** Visual progress tracker showing completed and remaining milestones
- **8 Onboarding Milestones:** Each milestone covers critical Adams Homes processes
- **Your Info:** Name, division, and onboarding progress

### Step 3: Complete Each Milestone (In Order)
**For each milestone, you'll:**
1. Read the description of what you're learning
2. Review key points and learning objectives
3. Watch the training video
4. Access resource materials
5. Mark as complete when finished

**Completion takes:** 10-15 hours total (spread over your first 1-2 weeks)

### Step 4: Ask Questions
If you don't understand something:
1. Go to the **"Questions"** section in your dashboard
2. Type your question
3. Submit it
4. Your admin will receive your question and respond
5. You'll get a response within 24 hours

### Step 5: Track Your Progress
- Your dashboard shows which milestones you've completed
- Check back regularly to stay on track
- Your manager can see your progress

## PART 8: TROUBLESHOOTING

### "I forgot my password"
- Go to login page and click "Forgot Password" (coming soon)
- For now, contact your administrator to reset

### "I can't access a resource link"
- Check that your work VPN is connected
- Try a different browser
- Contact your administrator if link is broken

### "I'm stuck on a milestone"
- Submit a question through your dashboard
- Your admin will respond within 24 hours
- Email your manager if urgent

### "I can't log in"
- Check that you're using your work email and correct password
- Try logging out and logging back in
- Contact your administrator for password reset
