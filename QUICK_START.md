# Quick Start - Admin Tasks

**Live Dashboard:** https://adams-homes-dashboard.vercel.app  
**Admin Portal:** https://adams-homes-dashboard.vercel.app/admin-login

---

## 🔑 Log In

1. Go to: https://adams-homes-dashboard.vercel.app/admin-login
2. Enter admin email
3. Enter admin password
4. Click "Login"

---

## ➕ Add One Employee

1. Click **"Associates"** tab
2. Click **"Add New Employee"**
3. Enter:
   - **Email** *(required)*
   - **Name** *(required)*
   - Division (optional)
   - Hire Date (optional)
4. Click **"Add Employee"**
5. **Copy the temporary password** shown
6. Share password with employee via secure method
7. Employee logs in and sets their own password

**Time:** 2 minutes per employee

---

## 📤 Bulk Add Employees (CSV)

1. Prepare CSV file (see format below)
2. Click **"Associates"** tab
3. Click **"Bulk Import"**
4. Select and upload CSV file
5. System shows:
   - ✅ How many imported successfully
   - ❌ Any errors (with row numbers)
6. **Download or copy the temporary passwords**
7. Send passwords to employees

**CSV Format:**
```
email,name,division,hireDate
john@example.com,John Smith,Austin TX,2026-06-20
jane@example.com,Jane Doe,Dallas TX,2026-06-22
```

**Time:** 10 minutes for 100 employees

---

## 📺 Configure Training Videos

1. Click **"Settings"** tab
2. Find the milestone (1-8)
3. Enter **Video URL** (YouTube, Vimeo, etc.)
4. Enter **Resource URL** (PDF, document link, etc.)
5. Click **"Save"**
6. ✅ Employees see it immediately

**Time:** 1 minute per milestone

---

## ❓ Answer Employee Questions

1. Click **"Questions"** tab
2. Read employee questions
3. Click the question to expand
4. Type your **Response**
5. Click **"Submit Response"**
6. Mark as **"Answered"**
7. ✅ Employee gets notification

**Time:** 5 minutes per question

---

## 👁️ Check Employee Progress

1. Click **"Dashboard"** tab
2. See:
   - Each employee's name
   - % complete for each milestone
   - Total progress
3. Click employee name for details

**Time:** 1 minute to review all

---

## 🔐 Employee Forgot Password

1. Click **"Associates"** tab
2. Find employee
3. Click **"Reset Password"**
4. Copy new temporary password
5. Send to employee via secure method
6. Employee logs in and creates new password

**Time:** 2 minutes

---

## ✏️ Update Employee Info

1. Click **"Associates"** tab
2. Click employee name
3. Click **"Edit"**
4. Update fields:
   - Lasso Login
   - Email Login
   - FPG Training URL
5. Click **"Save"**

**Time:** 1 minute per employee

---

## 🎯 Check Who's Behind

1. Click **"Dashboard"** tab
2. Look for employees with low % complete
3. Contact them to check in
4. Consider assigning milestone deadlines

**Time:** 5 minutes weekly

---

- [ ] Morning: Login and check Dashboard

**Time:** 15 minutes/day

- [ ] Wednesday: Update any training resources as needed

- [ ] Friday: Follow up with any struggling employees

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Employee can't login  
**Solution:** Reset password in Associates tab, send new temp password

**Problem:** Video URL not showing  
**Solution:** Verify URL in Settings, test link in new tab, resave, refresh page

**Problem:** Question not appearing  
**Solution:** Refresh page, make sure it's marked as "Pending"

**Problem:** Employee progress not showing  
**Solution:** Refresh dashboard, verify employee logged in

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

✅ Set a reminder to check Questions tab daily  
✅ Respond to questions within 2 hours  
✅ Use the CSV bulk import when adding 5+ employees  
✅ Test all video/resource URLs monthly  
✅ Add new hires on Day 1 of employment
